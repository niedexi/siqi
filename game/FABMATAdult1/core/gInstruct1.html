Before you start, please go to fullscreen.
If the game window appears too small, go to "View", click "Zoom in" to enlarge the window. If it appears too large, click "Zoom out".
Next, put your middle or index fingers on the E and I keys of your keyboard. Words or pictures will show up one by one in the middle of the screen. When the word/picture belongs to the group on the left, press the E key; when the word/picture belongs to the group on the right, press the I key. Words/pictures belong to only one group. If you make a mistake, an X will appear - fix the mistake by hitting the other key.


Press the space bar to begin.
