Before you start, please enter fullscreen.

If the game window appears too small, go to "View", click "Zoom in" to enlarge the window. If it appears too large, click "Zoom out".

Next, put your middle or index fingers on the E and I keys of your keyboard. Words will show up one by one in the middle of the screen. When the word belongs to the groups on the left, press the E key; when the word belongs to the group on the right, press the I key. Each word belongs to only one group.

If you make a mistake, an X will appear - fix the mistake by hitting the other key.

GO AS FAST AS YOU CAN.



Press the space bar to begin.
