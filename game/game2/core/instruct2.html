In this game, words and pictures will pop up on the screen. Your job is to sort these as quickly as you can while making as few mistakes as you can. You sort them by pressing the E button to put a word/picture in the left group, or by pressing the I button to put a word/picture in the right group.

You need to do this quickly but without making many mistakes! The game lasts about 5 to 10 minutes, so do your best!

Below, we show you important hints so you know how to sort the words and pictures into the right groups.

There are four different groups:

  • "Genius" group: the words "genius,", "brainy", "brilliant", "clever", "", "super-smart"
  • "Diligent" group: the words "diligent", "dedicated", "driven", "persistent", "hard-working"
  • "Math" group: pictures of Math
  • "Reading" group: pictures of Reading

IMPORTANT (please read carefully so you can play correctly):

  • Keep your middle or index fingers on the E and I keys so you can go quickly.
  • Two words at the top will tell you which words or pictures go with each key.
  • Each word or picture has a correct group. Most of these are easy.
  • Please try to go as quickly as you can, but without making many mistakes!
  • Focus as much as you can so you can get a great score!
